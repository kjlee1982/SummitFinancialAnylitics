What’s most helpful

Even rough numbers and a quick story go a long way. Particularly useful items include:

  • Approximate in-place income and major expenses
  • Recent rent roll and any T-12 or trailing financials
  • Current management situation and any immediate issues
  • Reason the opportunity exists (sale, recap, partnership, etc.)
  • Timing: how soon a decision or LOI is needed
